# Ticket Data Collector — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-29_

Ticket Data Collector ("the extension") is a private, internal tool used by our team to record ticket purchase information for our own record-keeping.

## What the extension does
When a team member is on a ticket purchase **confirmation page** on a supported ticketing website, the extension reads the order details shown on that page and sends them to a Google Sheet that the team member has configured in the extension's settings.

## Data collected
The extension only reads data that is already displayed on ticket confirmation pages on supported ticketing sites. This may include:
- Order details: event name, venue, date, section, row, seat numbers, quantity, price, and order number
- The email address associated with the ticket order

The extension does **not** collect browsing history, keystrokes, passwords, or payment card numbers.

## How data is used
Collected data is sent **only** to the Google Sheet / Google Apps Script endpoint that the team member configures in the extension's settings. It is used solely for the team's internal record-keeping of ticket purchases.

## Data sharing
We do **not** sell or share collected data with any third party. Data is transmitted only to the user-configured endpoint controlled by the team. It is not used for advertising, creditworthiness, or any purpose unrelated to the single purpose above.

## Data storage
The extension stores the user's settings and a temporary local queue of captured orders in the browser's local storage on the user's own device. The access key used to authenticate with the configured endpoint is stored only in the browser and is never sent anywhere except to that endpoint.
